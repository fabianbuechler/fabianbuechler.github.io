About me #

Fabian Matthias Büchler, born February 2nd 1984 in Mödling, Austria.

As passionate software engineer and system architect the quality of my work is key to me, as is the implementation of best practices of agile software development in my teams. That includes coding standards, test-driven development and user story mapping.

I'm working as a professional software engineer since over 15 years, primarily in the areas of web- and backend-development, preferably using Python and other open source software.

Privately I'm father of two daughters, part-time builder and spare-time outdoor enthusiast.
