About me

As passionate web developer and system architect, the quality of my work is key to me, in implementation, tests and documentation. I like to consequently think a project through from start to end, from idea to operation, from support to maintenance. Equally important to me is cross-project stategical development within the company.

Since nearly ten years I am working professionally as system architect and web developer, primarily on the backend side but also on the frontend side. In doing so I prefer to use open source software like Python.

Besides that I am enthusiastic father of two daughters - my most important project by far.
